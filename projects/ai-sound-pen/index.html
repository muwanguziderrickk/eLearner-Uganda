Project Description

In collaboration with SAENGDONG EDUCATION/HANGUL TO THE WORLD from the Republic of Korea, eLEARNER UGANDA is spearheading the implementation of AI Sound Pen Technology, a revolutionary device designed for language education. This initiative comes at a crucial time when the East African Community (EAC), including Uganda, is striving for regional integration and economic development.

On July 5th, 2023, the East African Community emphasized Swahili learning for regional unity. Uganda, directed by the Cabinet, integrates Swahili into its curriculum. AI Talking Pen Technology is introduced to enhance language education, supported by collaborative efforts for diverse learning needs.

eLEARNER UGANDA, in collaboration with Saengdong Education, is committed to promoting a culture of high-quality educational materials. Through the utilization of AI Talking Pen Technology, the initiative aims to provide engaging and accurate language learning resources. Beyond Swahili, the project endeavors to expand its scope to include Korean, Chinese, Russian, English, and other languages, thereby catering to diverse linguistic needs within Uganda's educational landscape.

Main Features

AI Talking Pen Technology: Innovative device specifically designed for language education.

Integration of Multiple Languages: Initial focus on Swahili with plans to expand to Korean, Chinese, Russian, English, and more.

Collaboration with International Partners: Partnership with SAENGDONG EDUCATION/HANGUL TO THE WORLD from the Republic of Korea to leverage expertise and resources

High-Quality Educational Materials: Commitment to providing engaging and accurate learning resources to support effective language acquisition.

Ease of Use: Intuitive design of the AI Talking Pen facilitates seamless integration into educational settings, enhancing teaching and learning experiences.

Learning Outcomes

  • Enhanced Language Acquisition: Improved access to high-quality educational materials and innovative technology aids in the effective learning of languages such as Swahili and beyond.
  • Promoting Regional Integration: By fostering proficiency in languages common to the East African Community, the initiative contributes to regional cohesion and cooperation.
  • Empowering Educators: Equipping teachers with advanced tools and resources enables them to deliver engaging and effective language instruction..
  • Supporting National Curriculum Goals: Integration of Swahili and other languages into the curriculum aligns with national directives to enhance language education in Uganda.
  • Advancing Educational Technology: The implementation of AI Talking Pen Technology showcases the potential of innovative solutions to address educational challenges and improve learning outcomes.

While the ministry is making good progress with the training of teachers with a new Swahili language syllabus, their task will be greatly improved with an easy to use technology device, such as the AI Talking Pen (Talking Pen). eLEARNER UGANDA with its partners Saengdong education is introducing a high quality publishing culture that pursues only lively and correct education to ease teaching and learning of languages. Additionally other languages to be taught and Publish high quality materials are Korean Language, Chinese, Russian, English and Swahili.
