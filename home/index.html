Welcome ! We're e-Learner Uganda

e-Learner Uganda was formed in 2012 with a vision to deliver education technology and associated solutions to the education sector in Uganda. At that time there was no clear approach from government for the widespread adoption of ICT and related technologies for learners and teachers in schools across the country, and with some notable exceptions computers in schools were largely absent.

Who we Are

e-Learner Uganda, established in 2012, is dedicated to delivering educational technology solutions to the Ugandan education sector. Initially, there was no clear government strategy for ICT adoption in schools. Today, the Ministry of Education and Sports is implementing a Digital Agenda to integrate EduTech solutions in schools and teacher training facilities. e-Learner Uganda collaborates with the ministry to standardize teaching methodologies, support the national curriculum with technology, and promote students' personal development. Embracing fourth industrial revolution technologies, including AI, we partner with global tech developers from countries such as South Korea and Austria to provide customized solutions for Ugandan schools.

Completed Projects

At e-Learner Uganda, we take pride in our successful projects, including the Computers 4 Kids initiative, which has equipped numerous schools with essential technology.
